About Josh

Design & Development Swiss Army Knife

With over 12 years of experience in product design and development, I am passionate about creating user-friendly and accessible solutions that address user challenges and meet business objectives. My career path has taken me from a Systems Engineer working on software and HMI screens for the Steel Industry; leading the design and development of marketing sites and a line of business application for an Environmental company, to crafting user experiences for early-stage growth companies.

Three years ago, I started at Relode as their first design hire. During that time, I worked in Figma to craft an extensive component library to support brand and development efforts for a handful of apps that support different user groups. Collaborating extensively with cross-functional teams, I worked to understand user goals and improve usability while contributing to the development process. In addition, I helped bring future product ideas, nebulous in nature, to life via mockups and prototypes to help brainstorm and tighten up product roadmap features and feasibility.

Previously at AES/Clean Earth, I served as the sole designer and developer for the company's line of business application, by transitioning a desktop application to the web. This monolith application, initially built in VB.net, incorporated web, business, and data layers; and was built using HTML, CSS, JS, and SQL backend. Since its inception in 2012, I spearheaded UI design and later managed a team of developers, while actively contributing to front-end, back-end, and database development. As the company expanded through acquisitions, so did the application's reach, soon to support over 50 facilities with revenue exceeding $800 million. It continues to be used today.

My Process

Typical end-to-end workflow.

  • Requirements
  • Research
  • Design
  • Handoff
  • Observe

Requirements

What's the goal?

Understanding the goal of a feature request in a product requiring UI/UX design is paramount for success. By delving deep into the purpose behind each request, I ensure that every design decision is aligned with the overarching objectives of the product and the needs of its users. Through user research and analysis, I gain clarity on the desired outcomes and how the proposed feature will contribute to enhancing the user experience. This approach allows me to work closely with product stakeholders to determine the best solution.

Research

Understand the User

With a keen focus on UI/UX design, I immerse myself in understanding user needs through surveys, interviews, and feedback sessions. This hands-on approach allows me to grasp user pain points and envision functionalities that resonate with the audience. Armed with these insights, I meticulously craft intuitive and visually captivating interfaces that seamlessly integrate into the product. Through this process, I not only meet but exceed user expectations, driving engagement and fostering long-lasting relationships with end users.

Design

Wireframe. Mockups. Prototypes. Oh My.

With the knowledge gained from the requirements and research phase, I translate ideas into wireframes, outlining the basic structure and functionality of the feature. Refining these wireframes into detailed mockups, I bring the design to life with visual elements and interface components. Finally, I develop interactive prototypes, allowing stakeholders to experience the feature firsthand and provide valuable feedback. Depending on the task or feature, all of these may not be needed. I only produce what's necessary.

Handoff

Pass the Baton

I work to ensure that mockups are comprehensive and clearly communicate the design vision to the development team. Through detailed annotations and specifications, I provide developers with the necessary guidance to accurately translate the design into code. This collaborative approach fosters open communication and alignment between design and development, ultimately leading to the successful implementation of the feature. By streamlining the design handoff process, I empower developers to bring the design to life while maintaining the integrity of the user experience, driving product innovation and success.

Observe

Monitor the Metrics

By leveraging analytics tools and user feedback mechanisms, I continuously track how users interact with the interface, identifying areas for improvement and optimization. Through heatmaps, user session recordings, and more, I gain invaluable insights into user behavior, preferences, and pain points. This data-driven approach allows me to refine and enhance the user experience iteratively, ensuring that the product evolves in alignment with user needs and expectations. By staying attuned to user engagement and satisfaction metrics, I drive continuous innovation and ensure that the product remains competitive and compelling in the market.
